Amir Hossein Mohammad Amri

MSc Student in Information & Communication Technology (Telecommunication)
Università degli Studi di Padova, Italy

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from K. N. Toosi University of Technology, one of Iran's top five engineering schools, where I focused on Telecommunications and Electronics alongside outstanding professors and peers. During this time I fell in love with programming (C/C++, Python, MATLAB) and embedded systems (ARM Cortex, FPGA).

I graduated in October 2025 with an MSc in Information & Communication Technology (Telecommunication) from the University of Padova, ranked among the top 150 universities worldwide. My master’s thesis, titled “Ultra-Wideband Channel Sensing Using Wi-Fi Signals”, focused on harnessing commodity Wi-Fi hardware for high-resolution sensing applications, combining wireless communications, signal processing and machine learning.

During the program I completed advanced coursework in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, 5G/6G Networks, and Advanced Wireless Communications. I also significantly strengthened my C++ expertise and mastered Git/version control in the "Programming for Telecommunication" course.

I’m passionate about the intersection of wireless communications, signal processing, and machine learning, and I’m actively looking for PhD/research opportunities in these areas.
