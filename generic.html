In my undergraduate studies, I studied electrical engineering at KNTU University, one of the top five engineering institutions in Iran. While being guided by eminent professors and in the company of diligent classmates, I immersed myself in Telecommunications and Electronic courses, areas of interest to me. The diverse projects undertaken during my studies exposed me to various software and programming languages, including C/C++, Python, and Matlab. I also found joy in programming electronic boards like ARM Cortex and FPGA boards.
For my master's degree, I am currently enrolled in Information Communication Technology (ICT) at the University of Padova, specializing in Telecommunication. This academic journey has included impactful courses such as Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Neural Networks, 5G, and Wireless Networks, providing me with a comprehensive understanding of diverse aspects within the field. Notably, in the "Programming for Telecommunication" course, I significantly expanded my proficiency in C++ programming language and gained expertise in utilizing the Git tool.
