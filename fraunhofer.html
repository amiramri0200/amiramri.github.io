Overview

During my Master's studies, I had the privilege of joining Fraunhofer IIS, a world-renowned research institute in Germany, for two research internship periods. My work focused on the intersection of telecommunications and artificial intelligence, specifically within the domain of 5G networks.

Phase 1: Network Simulation

Period: Feb 2024 - May 2024
In the initial phase of my internship, I focused on system-level simulations to analyze wireless network performance. My key contributions included:

  • Performed comprehensive system-level simulations for analyzing wireless network performance metrics.
  • Applied mathematical modeling and advanced algorithm development to optimize resource allocation strategies.

Phase 2: Wireless Communications & ML

Period: Jul 2025 - Sep 2025
Returning for a second term, I expanded my scope to integrate Machine Learning with network simulations. My key achievements included:

  • Designed and simulated 5G scheduling frameworks utilizing OMNeT++ and Python.
  • Developed a custom API to integrate OMNeT++ with OpenAI Gym, enabling Reinforcement Learning (RL) based LTE/5G scheduling.
  • Implemented complex network control algorithms combining classical signal processing with modern machine learning techniques.
